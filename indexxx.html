About the Author

Sarah Kende is a Papua New Guinean author and cultural storyteller dedicated to preserving the narratives of courage that transformed the Samberigi Polopa region. Her writing honors the traditional leaders whose endurance and faith led to the arrival of Christianity — illuminating both the struggles and triumphs of a people embracing change.
